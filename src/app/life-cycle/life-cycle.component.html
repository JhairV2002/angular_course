Life Cycle Components

Life cycle components are the stages of a component. Created to destroyed

First of all remember this whenever the component is renderized in the view it calls the constructor, after that, the onInit hook is called

constructor: is only for call services or initialized variables.

NgOnIinit: is only for logic.

We have this diferent hooks

  • ngOnChanges
  • ngOnInit
  • ngDoCheck
  • ngAfterContentInit
  • ngAfterContentChecked
  • ngAfterViewInit
  • ngAfterViewChecked
  • ngOnDestroy
